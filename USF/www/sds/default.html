Secure Document Store

Index, categorise and access your documents stored on the Cloud, your local filesystem or in a database.

Features:-
Multiple Categories - Different Categories for different document groups.
Categorised Tags - Define Tags for a particular group of documents.
Link Categories - Define "common" Tags to link documents across Categories.
Real or Virtual Documents - Store documents in a database or on a "disk".
Encrypt - Virtual or Real, grouped or associated: Encrypt them uniquely.
Manage - Move, delete, copy or rename any stored document.
